Custom Blythe Dolls

Blythe Dolls are fashion dolls with over-sized heads and big eyes which change colours with a pull of a string. They were first invented in 1972 and introduced in the USA market but only became popular in 2000 after they were recognized in Japan. A lot of doll hobbyists and photographers customize these dolls by creating their clothes, shoes, hairstyles and all sorts of details, some of whom sell them for a high price.
