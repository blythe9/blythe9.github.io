Neo Blythe is a fashion doll that her head is about the size of a grapefruit with slender body. She always dress up with clothes especially made for her. When you pull the string at the back of her head, her eye colours and eye direction will change and this changes her face expression totally. When her eye colour and direction change, she looks almost like a different doll.
