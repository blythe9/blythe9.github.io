About me

Hi! My name is Jo Chen. I am Australian and I now live in Adelaide, Australia, with my friends and my dolls in a three floor town house.
I graduated from the University of Sheffield, UK by obtaining a master degree. My major is Information System Management. Prior that I studied interior design in the Tongji University in Shanghai, China.
I have wide range of interests and I have been focus on how art, technology, and design can be combined to benefit people's life and influence creative industry. In the coming years, my wide range of interests combined with my in-depth IT expertise will enable me to contribute greatly to some fields. I look forward to solving many interesting problems both alone and in collaboration with others.
