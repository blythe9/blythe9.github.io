How to make a Black Blythe

20.08.2014

First of all get some SEM paint from Ebay USA, all other colour will peel off the legs (I tried it myself, trust me, it hurts when this happens to your Blythe after all that work!!). To prepare the parts, sand everything gently even the body with fine sanding paper. Wash the parts with oilfree soap and hang up the body somewhere outside. Shake your SEM well and spray parts and body carefully.
